About Company

Know about
Swift Health & Hygiene

Swift Healthcare & Hygiene is Kashmir's fastest growing intermediary organization in healthcare, personal hygiene products. Established in 2011, with head office placed in downtown of Srinagar, J&K India, Swift Healthcare & Hygiene has seen a humungous / phenomenal growth from a mere intermediary setup to well acclaimed C&FA, Super Stockist and in retail distribution as well. A passionate 12 year accomplishment in the super stockiest & distribution sector with an in-depth understanding of the complex and ever-changing retail universe, makes us a one-stop provider of all professional end-to-end marketing & distribution services. Swift Healthcare & Hygiene has over a period of time and with better learning built a pan J&k distribution network, despite of such a diversified and well acknowledged market behavior in each of the territory. We understand that the retail industry has become increasingly demanding as well as challenging (in terms of customer expectations) hence brands are looking for partners that delivers to all verticals of the trade. A recent, inclusion in food division has given us another positive impetus that “WE ARE WHAT WE BELIEVE” With an Established network of 100+ distributors, inclusive of sub-distributors, with presence in 12 districts and 50 towns of Jammu and Kashmir. Penetration: Covering General Trade, Modern Trade, Departmental Stores, Convenience Stores, Whole Sale, Food Service, On Line Retail, Pharmacy and Institutions.

Our Journey

Know about
Out Journey and stats

Journey Timeline

  • 2008 - MS New Bilal

    In 2008, we started our journey as M/S New Bilal with a modest investment of 10 Lakhs, specializing in orthopedic and surgical products.

  • 2012 - Swift Orthotics

    Since 2012, we have diversified our product range to include rehabilitation aids, hospital furniture, and personal hygiene products. We also started M/S SWIFT Orthotics to strengthen our super stockist business network.

  • 2016 - MS Al-Hayat

    In 2016, we integrated our business by launching M/S Al Hayat Healthcare, a sister concern dedicated to building a retail module that allows us to reach end customers directly. The following year, we started M/S Swift Trading to deepen our penetration into retail networks and reach distant markets, bridging gaps with end customers

  • 2023 - Swift Healthcare

    As we continue to evolve and grow, we are thrilled to announce the formation of Swift Healthcare & Hygiene Private Limited in 2023. This new entity is the culmination of our efforts to integrate all our previous businesses under one roof, making it the mother company for the entire Swift Group.

  • GOAL

    Our goal at Swift Healthcare & Hygiene Private Limited is to be the leading provider of high-quality healthcare, hygiene, and baby products in India and beyond. We strive to achieve this by offering a comprehensive product range, personalized solutions, and exceptional service, all while maintaining the highest standards of quality and integrity.

Our Mission

Know about
Out missions and goals

At Swift Healthcare & Hygiene Private Limited, our mission is to improve the health and well-being of our customers by providing them with high-quality healthcare, hygiene, and baby products. We aim to achieve this by staying committed to our values of integrity, quality, and innovation while ensuring that we offer personalized solutions that meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our ultimate goal is to be the go-to provider of healthcare and hygiene products in India and beyond, and to make a positive impact on the communities we serve

Our Vision

Out vision and principles

Our vision at Swift Healthcare & Hygiene Private Limited is to be the most trusted and leading group of marketing intermediaries in the industry. We are committed to building our business on a foundation of trust, integrity, and exceptional service to our internal and external customers. Our goal is to set the standard for excellence in our industry by providing innovative solutions, personalized support, and high-quality healthcare, hygiene, and baby products.
